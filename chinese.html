Dry Manchurian

Veg

Photo 2

Here our Recepies begins

Ingredients

    Carrot - 1 no. grated
Cabbage - 1 cup finely grated 
French Beans - 2 nos cut up
Capsicum - 1/2 no. finely chopped
Chopped Ginger - 1/2 tsp 
Chopped Garlic - 1/2 tsp 
Spring onion stalk - 2 tsp 
Spring onion - 2 tsp 
Salt to taste
Pepper 
All purpose flour/Maida - 3 tsp 
Corn flour - 2 tsp
Oil for frying

For Manchurian sauce
Oil - 2 tsp 
Chopped Ginger - 1/2 tsp 
Chopped Garlic - 1/2 tsp 
Spring onion - 1/4 cup
Spring onion stalk - 2 tsp 
Vinegar - 2 tsp
Soy sauce - 2 tsp 
Red Chili sauce - 3 tsp 
Tomato Ketchup - 2 tbsp 
Pinch of Salt 
Pepper powder - 1/4 tsp 
Little Water
Garnish it with Spring Onion stalk

Recepie

    1. In a mixing bowl add carrot, cabbage, french beans, capsicum, chopped ginger, chopped garlic, spring onion stalk, spring onion, salt, pepper,
    all purpose flour/maida and corn flour.
2. Mix well, vegetables leave to water knead to get a dough like mixture. 
    Note: If the vegetables did not bind add little all purpose flour/maida to bind them well.
3. Take some mixture and make them a even size balls. Keep aside.
4. Heat oil for frying in a kadai.
5. Add the vegetable balls and deep fry them till it turns crisp and golden color.
    Note: Keep the flame on low, it has to cook through otherwise uncooked from inside balls.
6. Heat oil in a pan. Add chopped garlic, chopped ginger, spring onion, spring onion stalk and saute for few minutes.
7. Now add vinegar, soy sauce, red chili sauce, tomato ketchup, salt, pepper powder and mix well. 
8. Then add little water to the sauce and cook for few mins
9. As the sauce thickens add the fried vegetable balls and coat it evenly.
10.Garnish it with spring onion stalk.
11.Serve the hot Dry Vegetable Manchurian as a starters and enjoy with your family and friends.

Fried rice

Here our Recepie begins

Ingredients

    Oil - 2 Tbsp
Garlic - 1 Tbsp Finely Chopped
Spring Onion Whites
Carrot - 1 No.
Green Beans
Cabbage - 1 Cup
Boiled Sweetcorn - 1/2 Cup
Baby Corn 
Salt - 3/4 Tsp
Pepper
Light Soy Sauce - 2 Tbsp
Vinegar - 1 Tbsp
Sugar - 1/4 Tsp
Spring Onion Greens

Recepie

    1. Cook the rice till its 3/4th done and strain it and keep it aside 
2. Heat oil and stir-fry garlic.
3. Saute onions, finely chopped vegetables on high flame
4. Add soy sauce, vinegar, sugar, pepper and salt. 
5. Mix well and saute 
6. Add cooked rice.
7. Mix everything until well combined
8. Add spring onion greens and turn off the stove 
9. Serve the fried rice hot.

Chowmein

Veg

Ingredients

    Ingredients:
Oil – 2 tbsp
Ginger chopped – 1 tsp
Garlic chopped – 1 tsp
Onion sliced – ½ cup
Cabbage shredded – 1 cup
Carrot julienne – ½ cup
Peppers shredded – 1 cup
Noodles boiled – 2 cups
Light Soya sauce – 2 tbsp
Dark soya sauce – 1tbsp
Green Chilli sauce –  1 tsp
Vinegar – 1 tbsp
Pepper powder – ½ tsp
Salt – to taste
Spring onions (chopped) – a handful

Recepie

    1. Heat a pan to high heat and add oil.
2. Add ginger & garlic and give a quick stir. Now add sliced onions, cabbage, carrots and peppers. 
3. Toss them for 2 minutes on high heat. Add boiled noodles, soya sauce, chilli sauce, vinegar, salt, pepper power and toss 
    the noodles for 3-5 mins. 
4. Sprinkle spring onions, mix and remove from heat. Serve immediately.

Chilli Chicken

NON-Veg

Ingredients

    For marination:

 Boneless chicken (preferably thigh) cut into 1 inch cubes- 300 gms 
 Salt- 1/2 tsp
 Crushed Pepper powder- 1/2 tsp
 Cornflour- 2 tbsp
 Maida or All purpose flour- 1 tbsp
 Soy sauce- 1 tsp
 Ginger garlic paste- 1 tsp
 Egg - 1

 Onion petals- 1 small onion
 Green capsicum cubed- 1 medium
 Garlic, chopped- 4-5 cloves
 Green chillies, thin slices- 2 nos
 Spring onion, white portion chopped- 2
 Spring onions, green tops- chopped for garnishing

 Red Chilli sauce- 2 tbsp
 Tomato sauce- 2 tbsp
 Soy sauce- 1 tbsp
 Vinegar- 1 tsp
 Cornflour slurry- 1 tbsp cornflour mixed in 2 tbsp of water
 Oil- 2 tbsp
 Salt for seasoning

Recepie

    Preparation:

 1.Wash and cut the boneless chicken into pieces.
 2.Marinate with the items indicated. Mix well and set aside for 30 mins.
 3.Chop the garlic and spring onions and slice the chillies.
 4.Make petals of one small onion & cubes of one green capsicum
 5.Deep fry the chicken pieces in a kadhai till golden in colour. Remove and set aside

Process:

 1.Take a small wok or kadhai & heat 2 tbsp oil.
 2.Add the chopped garlic, stir for a few seconds and add the white chopped spring onions.
 3.Stir it once & add the sliced green chillies.   Mix everything well and then add the onion and capsicum petals. Sauté on medium heat for around 
    2 mins till they are still crisp.
 4.Now add all the sauces as mix well. Stir fry for 1 min.
 5.Add the fried chicken pieces and mix well so that the pieces are coated with the sauce.
 6.Add the cornflour slurry and mix well again. Add little water if required.
 7.Cook for few mins & garnish with chopped spring onion greens.

Chicken Lollipop

NON-Veg

Ingredients

    - Chicken Winglets (around 10-12 pcs) - 500 gms. (Cut the meat from bone and push down to create a lollipop shape)

1st Marinade: 
 Soy Sauce- 1 tsp
 White Vinegar- 1.5 tsp
 Kashmiri Chilli Powder- 1 tsp
 Ginger Garlic Paste- 2 tsp
 Black Pepper Powder- 1/4 tsp
 Salt- 1/2 tsp 

2nd Marinade:
 Cornflour- 1/4 cup
 Maida/ All purpose flour- 3 tbsp
 Egg- 1
 Kashmiri Chilli Powder- 1.5 tsp
 Salt- pinch

Other Ingredients:
 Ginger, fine chopped- 1 tbsp
 Garlic, fine chopped- 1 tbsp
 Onion, fine chopped- 3 tbsp
 Spring onion greens, chopped - 2 tbsp
 Schezwan Sauce- 3 tbsp
 Soy Sauce- 1 tsp
 Tomato Ketchup- 2 tbsp
 Sugar - 1 tsp
 White Vinegar- 1 tsp
 Corn flour slurry- 3/4 tsp mixed in 4 tbsp water

Recepie

    Preparation:

1. Clean the chicken winglets, wash and shape them into lollipops.
2. Marinate the lollipops with the 1st marinade items. Mix well and set aside for 30 mins.
3. Now add all the 2nd marinade items, mix well and set aside. 
4. Fine chop the ginger, garlic, onion and chop the spring onion greens. 

Process:

1. Heat oil in a pan or skillet for deep frying the lollipops.
2. Drop the battered lollipops one by one in hot oil, to not crowd the oil. Do not move for 2 mins and then keep stirring to give a uniform golden 
    brown colour. 
    Keep frying for another 4-5 mins till the chicken is cooked and fried golden.
3. Remove onto a plate and repeat the same with the balance.
4. These fried lollipops can also be eaten at this stage. 

For making Drums of Heaven:

1. Heat oil in a wok and add the chopped ginger and garlic. Give a stir for 15 secs and then add the chopped onions. Sauté on high heat for a minute till 
   the onions are soft.
2. Now add the Schezwan Sauce, give a mix and then add the soy sauce. Again give a mix and add the tomato ketchup. 
3. Add the sugar and vinegar, give a mix and sauté on medium heat for a minute till the sauce is smooth and everything is mixed well.
4. Add the corn slurry and keep cooking it for another 30 secs till the sauce is thick and smooth.
5. Lastly add the lollipops fried earlier and stir fry it for a minute with the sauce to coat it well. Garnish with chopped spring onion greens, give a mix and 
    serve hot.

Dimsums

NON-Veg

Ingredients

    200 gms boneless chicken
ginger
garlic
salt
soy sauce
sesame oil
spring onions
pepper 
wonton sheet

Recepie

    Wonton wrapper 

1. Mix maida, salt, oil and water 
2. Mix it to a dough 
3. Knead it well for about 10 mins 
4. Let it rest for about 30 mins 
5. Roll it into thin wrappers 

Chicken Dim Sum 
1. Take minced chicken, add egg, light soy sauce and cornflour in a mixer-grinder. Mix it nicely, using a mixer-grinder.
2. Add other seasonings such as salt, meat masala and pepper.
3. Add onion and sesame oil and mix till soft.
4. Stuff the mixture in the wonton wrapper.
5. Arrange the dimsums in a basket and steam for 15 minutes.
6. Serve with red chilli chutney.
