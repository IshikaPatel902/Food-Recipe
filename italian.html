Panini sandwich

Veg

Ingredients

    Pesto 
2 cups - Packed fresh basil leaves
3 cloves - garlic
¼ cup - roasted pine nuts
½ cup - extra virgin olive oil
½ cup - grated pecorino romano cheese
as needed - salt & pepper

Grilled Vegetable 
4 Tbls - olive oil
1 small - ½ inch sliced Japanese or Chinese eggplant
1 small - ½ inch sliced zucchini
1 small - ½ inch sliced yellow squash
1 medium - ½ inch sliced red onion
1½ Tbls - Chef Brik’s “Black Garlic & Shallot” rub

as needed - Salt & Pepper

Sandwich
1 cup - basil leaves
2 ea - ½ inch sliced tomatoes
4 ea - roasted piquillo peppers
8 oz - sliced fresh mozzarella cheese (water packed)
4 ea - fresh ciabatta bread or baguette

Recepie

    1.Preheat grill over med-high heat
2.For Pesto: Place all ingredients in a food processor except the olive oil. Begin to puree & slowly add in the olive oil while processing. Season with 
   salt & pepper. 
3.For the grilled vegetables: in a large flat baking sheet, drizzle the sliced vegetables with olive oil & seasonings. Grill for 3-4 mins per side.
4.Sandwich assembly: take both pieces of the bread & spread with pesto and begin to assemble the sandwich. Wrap with foil & press on a grill for 
   4 mins per side, or use a panini press.

Risotto ball

Veg

Ingredients

    1 cup Arborio rice, boiled
1 small yellow capsicum, finely chopped
1 small green capsicum, finely chopped
2 tablespoons finely chopped fresh coriander leaves
Salt to taste
Crushed black peppercorns to taste
3 tablespoons grated parmesan cheese
2 tablespoons dried breadcrumbs + for coating
1 tablespoon grated cheddar cheese
1 tablespoon grated mozzarella cheese
¼ teaspoon red chilli flakes
1 ½ tablespoons cornflour
Oil for deep-frying
Arrabiata sauce for serving
Fresh basil leaves for serving

Recepie

    1. Mix together Arborio rice, yellow capsicum, green capsicum, coriander leaves, salt, crushed peppercorns and 2 tablespoons grated parmesan cheese 
    in a bowl. Add breadcrumbs and knead into a soft dough. 
2. Mix together cheddar cheese, remaining parmesan cheese, mozzarella cheese and chilli flakes in another bowl. Divide the mixture into equal 
    portions and shape into small balls. 
3. Divide the dough into equal portions. Flatten each portion, place a cheese ball in the centre and shape into lemon-sized balls.
4. Mix together cornflour and 2-3 tablespoons water to make slurry. 
5. Dip each ball in slurry, coat in breadcrumbs and set aside.
6. Heat sufficient oil in a kadai. Deep-fry the balls till golden brown. Drain on absorbent paper. 
7. Serve hot with Arrabiata sauce and basil leaves.

Saucy Pasta

Veg

Ingredients

    To Make White Sauce Macaroni

Macaroni - 1 1/2 Cups
Salt - 1 Tsp
Butter - 1 Tbsp
Oil - 1 Tsp
Onion - 1 No.chopped
Garlic - 5 Cloves Chopped
Capsicum
Pepper - 1/2 Tsp
Red Chilli Flakes - 1 Tsp
Italian Seasoning - 1 Tsp

To Make White Sauce

Butter - 1 1/2 Tbsp
All Purpose Flour - 2 Tbsp
Milk - 1 1/2 Cups
Salt - 11/4 Tsp
Pepper - 1/2 Tsp
Chilli Flakes - 1/2 Tsp
Mixed Herbs - 1/2 Tsp
Cheese - 2 Slices

Recepie

    For Cooking the Macaroni

1. Pour water in a saucepan.
2. Add macaroni shells and salt to it.
3. Cook the macaroni completely, strain and keep it aside.
4. To a wide pan, add butter, oil, onion and garlic. Saute for 1 minute.
5. After the onions turn transparent, add red , green and yellow bell peppers.
6. Mix everything and add salt, pepper powder, chilli flakes, Italian seasoning and give it a quick mix again.
7. To this add cooked macaroni, stir well, turn off the stove and keep aside.

For Making the White Sauce

1. To a wide saucepan, add butter and all purpose flour. Combine it well.
2. Pour milk to this mixture and mix it with a whisk to make sure no lumps are formed.
3. Keep the flame on medium low to slow cook the sauce.
4. Now  add salt, pepper powder, chili flakes, mixed herbs, mix well and let it cook.
5. Next add cheese slices and let it melt fully.
6. Check for seasoning and add if anything is required.
7. Once the cheese mixture becomes a bit thick and creamy, add the cooked macaroni to it and mix everything well.
8. Garnish with some chili flakes and freshly grated cheese.
9. Creamy & cheesy White sauce macaroni is ready to be served!

Bruschetta

Veg

Ingredients

    Baby tomatoes halved - 15-20
Baguette 
Extra virgin olive oil - 6 tablespoons
Garlic,chopped - 10 cloves
Salt  to taste
Jalapeno - 2
Black olives - 6-7
Mozzarella cheese grated - 4 tablespoons
Basil leaves - a few

Recepie

    1.Preheat oven to 200 °C / 400 °F. Heat olive oil in a non-stick pan. Add garlic and sauté on high heat until lightly browned. 
2.Add cherry tomatoes, salt and stir.
3.Cover and cook. Cut the baguette diagonally into thick slices. 
4.Thinly slice jalapeños and add the tomatoes and cover the pan again. Halve the black olives and add. 
4.Mix well and cover and cook till soft. Arrange the bread slices on a baking tray. Drizzle a little olive oil over them. 
5.Spread the tomato mixture on one end of the slices and mozzarella cheese on the other end. Place basil leaves over the cheese. Drizzle some more olive oil 
   over the slices. 
6.Keep the tray in the preheated oven and bake for 8-10 minutes or till the cheese melts. 
7.Serve hot

Lasagne

NON-Veg

Ingredients

    Chicken Mince – 250 gms
Olive Oil – 2 Tbsp
Garlic – 2 cloves minced
Onion – 1 small finely chopped
Tomato sauce – 1 jar, about 6 cups
Salt – 1 Tsp
Dried basil – 1 Tsp
Dried Oregano – 1 Tsp
Chicken Bouillon – 1
Water – 1 Cup

Ingredients for Béchamel sauce:

Butter – 3 Tbsp
All purpose flour – 3 Tbsp
Milk – 3 Cups
Salt & Pepper – to taste
Parmesan Cheese – 1/2 Cup
Chicken Bouillon – 1

Recepie

    1. Heat olive oil on medium heat. Add chopped garlic and onion, sauté well. Add salt.
2. Now add chicken mince, sauté until it turns white.
3. Add basil and oregano, mix well.
4. Add Tomato sauce, water and 1 chicken bouillon.
5. Cover & cook for 15 minutes and the meat sauce is ready. Keep aside.
6. Heat butter in another pan. Add flour and stir well.
7. Now slowly add milk and keep stirring to prevent forming lumps.
8. Add chicken bouillon, salt and pepper to taste.
9. Add parmesan cheese, bring it to a boil. Keep aside.
10. Now brush a baking pan with olive oil.
11. Add a layer of meat sauce, top it with no boil lasagna sheets, béchamel sauce, shredded mozzarella and parmesan cheese.
12. Now add another layer of no boil lasagna sheets, meat sauce, béchamel sauce, shredded mozzarella and parmesan cheese.
13. Sprinkle dried parsley and cover it with a foil.
14. Bake in a pre-heated oven at 200°C or 400°F for 45 minutes.
15. Remove the foil and bake for another 15 minutes.
16. Rest it for 15 minutes before serving.

Cheesy Pizza

NON-Veg

Ingredients

    For making pizza dough balls
Ingredients:
 Warm water 1/4th cup
 Sugar 1 tbsp
 Yeast 1.5 tsp
 Refined flour 2 cups
 Milk powder 1 tbsp
 Salt ½ tsp
 Warm water 1 cup
 Olive oil 1 tbsp

Assembly 
Ingredients:
 Pizza dough balls
 Maize flour as required for dusting
 Cheese sauce as required
 Readymade tortilla or homemade chapati 4 pcs, each for one pizza
 Pizza sauce as required
 Mozzarella cheese as required
 Toppings:
 Capsicum 
 Onions
 Tomatoes
 Sweet corn
 Paneer tandoori
 Oregano and chilli flakes as per taste

Recepie

    1.Pour warm water in bowl, add sugar and yeast, mix well and let the yeast froth up.
2.In a mixing bowl, add refined flour, milk powder and salt, mix well and add warm water slowly and gradually, and the yeast mixture, mix well and knead 
  into a soft dough, further add olive oil and knead until soft and smooth, you can also use electric blender for a minute for better results. 
3.Apply a little more olive oil on the dough surface to prevent it from drying, cover it with a cloth and rest for 1.5 hours in a warm place for the dough to 
   proof.
4.After proofing, puncture the air in the dough by kneading once again and smoothen the dough, divide into four equal parts, and tuck inwards to smoothen 
  the dough surface and apply oil once again to prevent it from drying, rest them as well for 15-20 minutes. Keep aside, to be used later for making freshly 
  baked pizza.

5.Sprinkle some maize flour and flatten the dough balls with hands into a flat pizza bread.
   By the time you make the pizza, Preheat the oven at 250℃
6.Use a plate and sprinkle some maize flour and place the pizza bread over it, shape the sides as shown in the video, further spread the cheese sauce, place a 
  ready-made tortilla or homemade chapati, further apply pizza sauce, sprinkle enough amount of mozzarella cheese and place the toppings, you can use toppings 
  as per your preference.  
7.Slide in your pizza over the hot baking tray and bake it at 250℃ or 10-15 minutes.
8.Your freshly baked cheese burst pizza is ready to be served, sprinkle some oregano and chilli flakes to enhance the taste and slice into as many slices you 
   would like to share with your mates. Make sure it’s hot and don’t end up burning your precious palate.
